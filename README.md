# time-box

Git-style version control for your calendar.

## What is time-box?

time-box brings the power of version control to your calendar. Just like Git tracks every change to your code, time-box tracks every change to your calendar, giving you full undo, redo, and history for all your calendar edits.

Connect your Google Calendar, and time-box captures a versioned snapshot every time something changes. Browse your full edit history, roll back to any previous state, and never lose track of how your schedule evolved.

## Core Features

### 🔄 Calendar Version Control
- **Full edit history** - Every calendar change is tracked as a versioned snapshot
- **Undo & redo** - Instantly revert or reapply changes to your calendar
- **Point-in-time restore** - Go back to any previous state of your calendar
- **Diff view** - See exactly what changed between any two versions

### 📅 Google Calendar Integration
- Authenticate with your own Google Calendar
- Real-time sync of calendar events and changes
- Non-destructive: your original calendar data is always preserved

### 📝 Daily Review & Time Reflection
- **Plan vs. reality** - Enter your original plan for the day, then review what actually happened
- **Easy adjustments** - Mark events as longer, shorter, skipped, or shifted (e.g., "commute took 20 min longer")
- **Time artifacts** - Generate a clear record of exactly how you spent your time each day
- **Reflection tools** - Understand where your time goes and identify areas for improvement

## Philosophy

time-box is built on one core belief: **your calendar should have the same safety net as your code.**

The versioning system is the foundation. Once version control for calendars is rock solid, it unlocks the next layer: AI that can reason about your calendar and time without messing things up, because every change is tracked and reversible.

## Roadmap

### Phase 1: Open Source & Local-First (current)
- Open source, downloadable tool
- Users authenticate their own Google Calendar
- Core versioning engine: snapshots, undo/redo, history browsing
- Daily plan vs. review workflow
- Focus on making it a great personal tool first

### Phase 2: Broader Accessibility
- Streamlined setup for tech-savvy users (CS students, developers, etc.)
- Easy Google account connection flow
- Community feedback and iteration

### Phase 3: Consumer Product
- Web app + mobile app
- Voice-powered calendar editing with full version control
- Edit your calendar on the fly with complete history and safety
- Accessible to anyone, not just developers

### Phase 4: AI-Powered Calendar Intelligence
- AI tools for reasoning about time and schedules
- Smart suggestions based on your time-spending patterns
- Automated review and reflection insights
- Built on the rock-solid versioning foundation
